Project Abstract

VisionSafe is an advanced AI-powered safety monitoring system designed to mitigate workplace hazards in industrial environments. Leveraging state-of-the-art Computer Vision algorithms (YOLOv8) and deep learning, the system analyzes live video feeds in real-time to detect Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) compliance with over 95% accuracy.


By automatically identifying missing helmets, vests, gloves, and safety gear, VisionSafe reduces the reliance on manual inspections and provides instantaneous alerts to site supervisors. This proactive approach minimizes accident risks, ensures regulatory compliance, and fosters a safer working culture.
